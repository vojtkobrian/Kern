Where Are We At?

A Brief History

Kern Brother's was founded by Henry Kern in 1943.
He started out brewing his own beer and selling it. With little success, he then turned Kern Brothers into a distributor, selling hundreds of different kinds of beer! Kern owned the business until 1985 when he sold it.
